标题:Algebro-geometric solutions of the coupled modified Korteweg-de Vries hierarchy
作者:Geng, XG (Geng, Xianguo); Zhai, YY (Zhai, Yunyun); Dai, HH (Dai, H. H.)
来源出版物: ADVANCES IN MATHEMATICS  卷: 263  页: 123-153  DOI: 10.1016/j.aim.2014.06.013  出版年: OCT 1 2014

摘要:Based on the stationary zero-curvature equation and the Lenard recursion equations, we derive the coupled modified Korteweg-de Vries (cmKdV) hierarchy associated with a 3 x 3 matrix spectral problem. Resorting to. the Baker-Akhiezer function and the characteristic polynomial of Lax matrix for the cmKdV hierarchy, we introduce a trigonal curve with three infinite points and two algebraic functions carrying the data of the divisor. The asymptotic properties of the Baker-Akhiezer function and the two algebraic functions are studied near three infinite points on the trigonal curve. Algebro-geometric solutions of the cmKdV hierarchy are obtained in terms of the Riemann theta function. (C) 2014 Elsevier Inc. All rights reserved.
